NP Membership

This membership is tailored for non-profit organizations in the Lagos business community, offering benefits that cater to their specific needs. These include reduced membership fees, access to networking events focused on the non-profit sector, and specialized resources to support their missions and activities.

Cost

$0.00 No cost

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership is an introductory plan for individuals or small businesses interested in joining the Lagos Chamber of Commerce. It provides essential access to resources, events, and services at a budget-friendly price, making it a great option for startups or those new to the chamber who want to explore its advantages before opting for higher membership levels.

Cost

$200 per year

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership is a mid-level option that provides more comprehensive benefits than the Bronze plan. It offers increased access to events, expanded networking opportunities, and a wider array of resources designed to support the growth of businesses and individuals. This tier offers an excellent balance between affordability and benefits, making it ideal for those seeking to deepen their engagement with the chamber.

Cost

$350 per year

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership is the top-tier plan, providing the most benefits and exclusive access to chamber resources. Members at this level receive invitations to VIP events, exclusive content, and personalized support to maximize their membership. This option is perfect for businesses and individuals aiming for high-level involvement and opportunities within the Lagos Chamber of Commerce.

Cost

$500 per year
