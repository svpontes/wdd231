Estevas Garden Snack Bar

Estevas Garden

Snack Bar

This name was selected because it reflects the inviting atmosphere and enticing aromas of freshly brewed coffee and baked goods. The name is easy to remember and aligns well with the café's identity once it is located in a park whose predominant flower is the Estevas flower

Site Purpose

The website will provide customers with essential information about Estevas Garden Snack Bar, including menu highlights, location, and hours of operation.

Scenarios

  • How can I make a reservation for a table at the café?
  • What are the café's operating hours and location?

Color Schema

  • Green (#333300): This warm, earthy tone will be used for headings and accents throughout the website. It reflects the natural, grounded feeling of a coffee shop, evoking a sense of calm and connection to nature, similar to the tones of coffee beans and organic ingredients.

  • To be used in: Backgrounds and some h1, h2

  • orange (#ff4401): This vibrant orange will serve as the background color. It adds a pop of energy and modernity, making the content stand out. The bold contrast between the green and orange creates a lively, inviting atmosphere, perfect for drawing attention to the café's unique offerings while maintaining a friendly and engaging aesthetic.

  • To be used in: Backgrounds form page

Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif for body text, ensuring readability and simplicity.

Secondary Font: Poppins, serif for headings to give a classic, modern elegant feel.

Wireframe

A wireframe sketch of the homepage layout will feature a hero image with the café's name and logo at the top, followed by sections for menu highlights, location, hours of operation, and a call-to-action for reservations and newsletter sign-up.

Mobile View: The layout will stack vertically for easy navigation on small screens.

Desktop View: The layout will feature design like landing regular page.
